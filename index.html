Our Story

The Neighborhood Spot

Conlon's Pub is Fort Worth's beloved neighborhood hangout — where the owner greets you by name, the bartenders shake your hand, and every night feels like a gathering of old friends. Old school vibe, zero pretension.

🎯

Games Galore

Challenge your crew to pool, ping pong, darts, or foosball. We've got all the classics to keep the good times rolling.

🎵

Perfect Vibes

Great music on the jukebox — loud enough to enjoy, quiet enough to have a real conversation. That's the sweet spot.

🤝

Community Hub

From F1 watch parties to casual meetups, Conlon's brings Fort Worth together. Everyone's welcome at the bar.
